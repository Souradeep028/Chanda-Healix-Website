About Our Products

Actiogen products are tested for toxicity and efficacy in a manner consistent with the rules and regulations of the Food and Drug Administration. Consequently, product development includes in vitro and in vivo testing, third party confirmation of basic science findings, and evaluation by qualified physicians at prominent universities around the globe. Actiogen focus since its inception has been on the forefront of the burgeoning fields of bio-engineered peptides and dermaceutical science. To that end, it has created new procedures and techniques that have yielded substantial advances in dermaceutical science and wound care. Actiogen was developed to bring to market products that are based on novel peptide chemistry. Actiogen’s principle focus to date has been in the development of products for the skin care, dermaceutical and wound care industries.
