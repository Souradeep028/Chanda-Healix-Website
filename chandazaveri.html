Chanda
Zaveri

Founder Actiogen

As founder and president, Ms. Zaveri is the driving force behind all developments at Actiogen. A molecular biologist and protégé to two-time Nobel Laureate Dr. Linus Pauling; Ms. Chanda Zaveri is the pioneer in the evolution of the biotechnology industry. Her most recent contributions include innovative developments in cosmeceuticals, nutraceuticals, wound-healing technology and cancer chemotherapeutics.

Ms. Zaveri received a Masters degree in Molecular Biology from UCSB. Subsequent graduate studies were undertaken at Cal Tech. After working with a major pharmaceutical corporation in Southern California, Ms. Zaveri chose to pursue her own scientific visions as an entrepreneur. Her subsequent accomplishments have proven her steadfast commitment to scientific integrity and global vision for the application of her research findings. The respect enjoyed by Ms. Zaveri among her scientific and medical colleagues rightly confirms her status as an innovative and leading personality in the expanding world of biotechnology. Ms. Zaveri has worked diligently to unite her research findings with marketable applications in cosmetics and nutriceuticals. Ms. Zaveri’s ongoing goal is to build a company that specializes in peptide chemistry and pharmaceutical innovations.

Get out of your safety cocoon.

Ms. Zaveri’s initial investigation into the skincare industry started in 1993 with the creation of a biologically active peptide that stimulated collagen synthesis. This technology served as the sole intellectual property asset of Ms. Zaveri’s initial corporate endeavor, which began in 1994 as Geneda Corporation. After moving onwards in 1998 to create the second advanced generation as Activor Corporation, Ms. Zaveri continued her work in skincare and shifted her focus towards the evolving science of biologically active wound healing products creating Angiomead Corporation and the most advanced third generation of B2-Actigen called CH+D Peptide produced by Actiogen Corporation.

Actiogen products are tested for toxicity and efficacy in a manner consistent with the rules and regulations of the Food and Drug Administration. Consequently, product development includes in vitro and in vivo testing, third party confirmation of basic science findings, and evaluation by qualified physicians at prominent universities around the globe.

Actiogen focus since its inception has been on the forefront of the burgeoning fields of bio-engineered peptides and dermaceutical science. To that end, it has created new procedures and techniques that have yielded substantial advances in dermaceutical science and wound care. Actiogen was developed to bring to market products that are based on novel peptide chemistry. Actiogen’s principle focus to date has been in the development of products for the skin care, dermaceutical and wound care industries.

Actiogen is bringing innovative Skin Care and Hair Wellness products directly to you for your "home spa" needs. Our products are the results of the research of Dr. Chanda Zaveri , a renowned chemist and molecular biologist, and have been tested and proven effective by independent laboratory studies. Our products use exclusive patented formulas utilizing our proprietary Peptide Chemistry. They are more effective than other products which use shared commercially available peptides that are found in other skin care lines. These formulations are combined with vitamins, minerals, and herbal and plant extracts which work in synergy to produce superior skin and hair care anti-aging treatments. We welcome you to share our success in Skin Care and Beauty through Advanced Scientific Research and to personally benefit from the innovative developments of Actiogen.
